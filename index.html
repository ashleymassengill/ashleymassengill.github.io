about

I am a UI Developer with a unique eye for editing, experience writing HTML5, CSS (Sass compiled), building in Drupal, and the skills needed to make great design. I specialize in ensuring design meets the needs of the end user and the user experience is the best it can be from all directions. I'm always working to expand my knowledge by taking online courses, testing new code, and playing with new designs.

I care about detail and considering the end user in all aspects of my work. I apply logic and careful consideration to design and excel at asking the hard questions to improve user experience. I take a minimalist approach to visual design and database build. My goal is to always present the best possible product for every aspect of use.

I'm also passionate about Krav Maga, kayaking, my dogs, books, and cake.
