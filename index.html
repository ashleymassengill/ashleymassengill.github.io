about

I am a product advocate and former UI developer with a unique eye for editing, experience writing HTML5, CSS (Sass compiled), building in Drupal and Wordpress, and the skills needed to make great design. I specialize in ensuring design meets the needs of the end user and the user experience is the best it can be from all directions.

As an accessibility subject matter expert, I care about detail and considering the end user in all aspects of my work. I apply logic and careful consideration to design and excel at asking the hard questions to improve user experience. My goal is to always present the best possible product for every aspect of use.
