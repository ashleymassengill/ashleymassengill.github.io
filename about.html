who am i?

I am a native North Carolinian, the odd ball of my family, downtown Raleigh aficianado, and self proclaimed snob. I am passoinate about my interests and I have a lot of interests.


McGee is my seven year old Border Collie who is spoiled to the core, too smart for my own good, and a forced cuddle lover. I will argue with anyone who doesn't think he's the most awesome.


I think food is a language of it's own and cooking is an art form. Flavors layer in your mouth the way colors layer on canvas. And the smell of certain dishes are more comforting than a good pair of sweats.


Culture is entertainment, and absorbing it keeps me centered. Ballet is poetry, music is color, art is genius. Sports are energy. Books are peace.
